# Executive Summary

## Project Name

Supply Sentinel

## One-Line Description

Supply Sentinel is an early-warning AI agent that detects external supply risks and translates them into internal business impact, including affected products, customers, plants, inventory days, and recommended first actions.

## Problem

Supply risks such as raw material shortages, logistics disruption, and supplier delays affect procurement, production, sales, and customer response. In many companies, the signals are scattered across news, supplier emails, PDF notices, market data, and individual judgment.

As a result:

- Detection is late.
- Impact scope is unclear.
- Initial response depends on specific people.
- Prioritization takes time.
- Management reports are prepared manually.

## Solution

Supply Sentinel periodically monitors external signals, extracts supply-risk events with AI, and compares them with internal data such as inventory, BOM, alternatives, and orders.

When risk is high, it automatically prepares:

- Impacted products
- Impacted customers
- Impacted plants
- Estimated days until shortage
- Risk score
- Recommended initial actions
- Teams alert
- Management report

## Core Value

The value is not just detecting that "naphtha may be short in the market."

The value is answering:

> Does this matter to our company, which products are exposed, and what should we do first?

## Hackathon Positioning

Supply Sentinel is not a generic supply chain optimization tool. It is an early-warning and first-response agent.

Traditional supply chain AI often optimizes internal demand, inventory, logistics, or production plans. Supply Sentinel focuses on external signals that indicate a crisis before internal operations visibly break.

## MVP Scenario

The demo uses a naphtha supply-risk scenario.

1. External news reports naphtha supply instability.
2. Supplier notice mentions delay or allocation.
3. AI extracts risk details.
4. Internal inventory and BOM are checked.
5. Affected products and customers are identified.
6. Remaining inventory days and risk score are calculated.
7. Teams alert and management report are generated.
8. Human approvers decide actual business actions.

## Winning Message

Supply Sentinel translates external supply risks into internal business impact and accelerates first response.
